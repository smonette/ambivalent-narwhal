About Us

ABC Blacksmith is the metalwork studio of Alex B. Carter, sculptural artist and blacksmith. Alex studied at the Sanders Art Institute and worked in Italy before opening ABC Blacksmith in 1999. Alex specializes in large-scale decorative and functional artworks in iron and brass. His sculptures find new lives as headboards, gates and railings and add unique flavor to your home and garden.
