Print Design
Orchesis Dance Poster
This poster was created for Cal Poly's Orchesis Dance Company, for their upcoming show "Immersion" scheduled for winter 2011. I hoped to express the feeling of a dancer immersed in the sound of the music they're dancing along to.
Masque of the Red Death Book
This handmade book is a typographic narrative of Edgar Allan Poe's classic "The Masque of the Red Death." It is an accordian fold book, with a soft-cover.
Pomegranate Identity
A rebranding for the Natural Foods Co-Op, a locally owned store in San Luis Obispo, Cal ifornia. This rebranding included a new name, logo and color palatte, as well as new business collateral.



Special Diets Tabletopper
This 5x7 tabletopper was created for Campus Dining in Fall 2010. It is meant to be displayed in different restaurants to inform diners of new foods to fit special dietary needs.
Kit Hinrichs Essay
This editorial layout of Kit Hinrich's essay"Telling Stories Visually." It is set in Museo and Helvetica, and the images included are famous magazine covers that Hinrichs references in his essay.

About Steph Monette

My name is Steph Monette and I'm the UI Developer and Designer at ModCloth in San Francisco. I have a BFA in Graphic Design from Cal Poly San Luis Obispo. My loves are beautiful letterforms, well-designed books, and bright colors. I'm well versed in HTML/CSS, JQuery, the Adobe CS5 Suite, Microsoft Office and can use both Mac and PC fluently.


In my free time I enjoy crocheting, hiking, kayaking, cooking, watching movies, and exploring San Francisco. Breakfast burritos might be my favorite food EVER.
